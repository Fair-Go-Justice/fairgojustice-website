Did You Know?

Important facts about Australia's justice system that every citizen should know.

$45,000+

The average cost of legal representation in a civil matter. Many families spend their entire savings on legal fees.

850,000

Australians who cannot afford legal representation and don't qualify for legal aid. They face the system alone.

18+ Months

Average wait time for civil matters to reach trial. Justice delayed is justice denied.

No Commission

Australia has no federal Judicial Integrity Commission. When judges misbehave, there's often no remedy.

600% Increase

In self-represented litigants over 20 years. More Australians are forced to navigate the system without help.

18 of 36

Australia's ranking among OECD countries for access to civil justice. We can and must do better.
