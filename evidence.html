Reform Hub

The evidence experience resolves into solutions. The Vault educates first — then points every visitor toward the three national reforms that can fix the system for all Australians.

🛡

Judicial Integrity Commission

Independent oversight of judicial conduct with real teeth, public transparency, and enforceable accountability standards.

  • Independent investigative powers
  • Public reporting requirements
  • Enforceable conduct standards
📹

Evidence Integrity Protocols

Mandatory bodycam retention, metadata standards, unbroken chain of custody requirements, and automatic disclosure obligations.

  • Mandatory bodycam retention periods
  • Unbroken chain of custody laws
  • Automatic disclosure timelines
⚖

Legal Aid Reform

Equal access to justice regardless of wealth or social status. Funded representation in all matters where liberty or family are at risk.

  • Universal access to representation
  • Independent funding model
  • Quality assurance standards
