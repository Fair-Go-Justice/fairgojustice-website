— Make Them Listen

WRITE TO
YOUR MP

A personalised email to your local MP carries far more weight than a social media post. Here's a template. Copy it. Send it. Make it yours.

01

Find your MP

Visit aph.gov.au/Members and search by your postcode. Take note of their email address.

02

Copy the template

Hit the button below. The full letter text is copied to your clipboard — ready to paste into your email client.

03

Personalise it

Add your name at the top and bottom. Change [Your state] to your actual state. Add a sentence about why this matters to you personally — it makes a difference.

04

Send it — and tell us

Hit send. Then come back and submit your story below so the community knows you stood up.

05

Did you get a response?

Share your MP's response — good or bad. Accountability starts with sunlight. Email us the response.

Subject: Urgent call for justice system reform — three proposals for your consideration


Dear [Your MP's Name],

I am writing to you as a constituent in [Your state] to respectfully but firmly ask you to champion three critical reforms to Australia's justice system.

Too many Australians have experienced a justice system that is opaque, inaccessible, and unaccountable. Evidence goes missing. Police misconduct goes unchecked. Ordinary people cannot afford representation. This is not good enough.

I am asking you to publicly support the following three reforms:

1. A National Judicial Integrity Commission — an independent body with real powers to investigate misconduct across all levels of the judiciary and law enforcement.

2. Mandatory Evidence Integrity Protocols — compulsory bodycam footage, strict chain-of-custody requirements, and independent auditing of all evidence handling.

3. Genuine Legal Aid Reform — restore and expand legal aid funding so that the right to a fair hearing is not determined by the size of your bank account.

These are not radical demands. They are the minimum a modern democracy owes its citizens.

I would appreciate your response on whether you support these proposals and what action you are prepared to take.

Yours sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your suburb, state]
