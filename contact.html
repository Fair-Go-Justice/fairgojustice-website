Frequently Asked Questions

How can I sign the petition?

Visit our petition page to add your signature. It takes less than a minute and helps build momentum for reform.

Is my story kept confidential?

Yes, absolutely. When you submit a story, you can choose whether to share it publicly (with or without your name) or keep it private. We never share personal details without explicit consent.

How can I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers! Send us a message using the contact form above with "Volunteer" as the subject, and tell us about your skills and availability.

Do you provide legal advice?

No, Fair Go Justice is an advocacy organization, not a legal service. We cannot provide legal advice. For legal assistance, please see our resources page for links to legal aid and community legal centres.
