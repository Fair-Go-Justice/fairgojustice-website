The Three Reforms

THE PILLARS OF CHANGE

Three evidence-based national reforms that would transform justice outcomes for every Australian.

01
🛡

JUDICIAL INTEGRITY COMMISSION

An independent body with real powers to investigate judicial conduct, conflict of interest, and systemic bias — with full transparency and public reporting.

Read the Proposal →
02
📹

EVIDENCE INTEGRITY PROTOCOLS

Mandated tamper-evident bodycam recording, real-time chain of custody tracking, and independent audit of all evidence from collection to verdict.

Read the Proposal →
03
⚖

LEGAL AID REFORM

Equal access to quality legal representation regardless of income. Self-represented litigants should not face corporations with unlimited legal budgets in silence.

Read the Proposal →
