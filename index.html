Our Framework

Three Reforms. One Fair Justice System.

Evidence-based reforms that can transform Australia's broken justice system — for every person, not just the privileged.

01

Judicial Integrity Commission

An independent, fully-funded National Judicial Integrity Commission — accountable, transparent, and free from political interference.

Read More
02

Universal Legal Aid Access

Expanded legal aid so every Australian facing criminal or civil proceedings gets fair representation — income should never determine justice.

Read More
03

Evidence Integrity Protocols

Mandatory metadata logging, chain-of-custody standards, and whistleblower protections to stop evidence from being buried, altered, or ignored.

Read More
The Full Picture

Everyone Deserves a Fair Go

Australia's justice system is meant to protect all of us. For hundreds of thousands, that promise has been broken.

The Problem

If you can't afford a $45,000 retainer, you're left to navigate a complex legal system alone. Evidence mysteriously "disappears." Court documents get backdated. Justice becomes a luxury.

The Movement

We're building a nationwide coalition demanding systemic reform. From students to retirees, workers to professionals — thousands standing together for accountability.

The Solution

Three evidence-based reforms: A National Judicial Integrity Commission, expanded legal aid access, and mandatory evidence integrity protocols. Detailed. Costed. Ready.
