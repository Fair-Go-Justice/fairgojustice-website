How We Make This Podcast

Powered by 256 Verified Sources

Every episode of The Fair Go Podcast is generated directly from verified court documents, affidavits, exhibits, and official records — using Google NotebookLM's AI audio technology. No guesswork. No fabrication. Every claim is traceable to a source document.

01

256 Source Documents

Court records, affidavits, expert reports, and case files loaded into a verified knowledge base.

02

AI Audio Overview

Google NotebookLM generates a natural conversation analysing the documents — cited, grounded, evidence-based.

03

Reviewed & Released

Episodes are reviewed for accuracy, then published across YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Podcasts.

04

Shareable Clips

Key moments become 60-second Shorts for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube — driving traffic back to the full episode.
