Class Action Information: Join the Fight for Justice

If you've been affected by systematic fraud, VIN tampering, or document falsification, you may be eligible to join our class action. Learn more about the process and how to register your interest.

What is a Class Action?

A class action is a legal proceeding brought by one person or a small group of people on behalf of a larger group (the 'class') who have similar claims against the same defendant(s). It's a powerful tool for individuals to seek justice when they've been wronged by systemic misconduct.

Why a Class Action?

  • **Collective Power:** It allows many individuals with smaller claims to collectively pursue a case that might be too expensive or complex to litigate individually.
  • **Efficiency:** It streamlines the legal process by resolving many similar claims in a single proceeding.
  • **Access to Justice:** It provides a pathway for individuals who might otherwise be unable to afford legal representation to seek redress.

Who Can Join?

You may be eligible to join this class action if you are a current or former driver or mechanic employed by or contracted with [Defendants], or a policy holder with [Insurance Company Defendant], and you have experienced one or more of the following since approximately 2015:

  • Operated or serviced vehicles that were not maintained in a safe, roadworthy condition.
  • Suffered financial loss (e.g., wage deductions), reputational damage, or emotional distress due to vehicle incidents or subsequent investigations.
  • Were subjected to blame-shifting, document backdating, or evidence tampering by [Defendants].
  • Were involved with vehicles suspected of VIN tampering or illegal rebirthing.

How to Register Your Interest

Registering your interest is a crucial first step. It helps us understand the scale of the issue and ensures you are kept informed of key developments without any obligation.

  1. **Register Below:** Fill out the form below to register your interest in joining the class action.
  2. **Share Your Story:** Visit our Share Your Story page to provide details of your experience. This is confidential and helps us build a robust case.
  3. **Contact Us:** If you prefer, you can contact our legal team directly to discuss your situation.
  4. **Stay Updated:** Subscribe to our Updates page for the latest news on the class action.

By registering your interest, you are not committing to anything but taking a vital step towards accountability and justice.
