What We're Demanding

This petition calls for a Royal Commission to investigate and address systemic failures in Australia's justice system.

National Judicial Integrity Commission

An independent body with real power to investigate judicial misconduct and ensure accountability at all levels.

Expanded Legal Aid

Adequate funding so every Australian can access quality legal representation, regardless of income.

Evidence Integrity

Mandatory protocols to prevent evidence tampering, ensure proper disclosure, and maintain chain of custody.
