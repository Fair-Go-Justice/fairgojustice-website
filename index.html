The Three Reforms

What We Are Fighting For

Three targeted, evidence-based reforms that would transform the Australian justice system — and restore public trust in our courts.

01
🛡

Judicial Integrity Commission

An independent body with real investigative powers to receive and investigate complaints against judicial officers — free from political influence.

Learn more →
02
📹

Evidence Integrity Protocols

Mandatory tamper-proof chain-of-custody standards for all evidence — body camera footage, documents, exhibits — in every Australian court.

Learn more →
03
⚖

Legal Aid Reform

Expanded, properly funded legal aid so that the quality of your justice is never determined by the size of your bank account.

Learn more →
